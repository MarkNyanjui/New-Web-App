Welcome to the World of Odyssey

Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action RPG set in Ancient Greece during the Peloponnesian War (431–422 BC). You play as either Kassandra or Alexios, a Spartan mercenary descended from the legendary King Leonidas.

Quick Facts

  • Developer: Ubisoft Quebec
  • Release Date: October 5, 2018
  • Platforms: PS4, Xbox One, PC, Nintendo Switch
  • Setting: Ancient Greece, 431 BC
  • Main Characters: Kassandra / Alexios

Story Overview

The story begins on the island of Kephallonia, where your character works as a mercenary. After a chance encounter leads to a contract to fight in the Peloponnesian War, you are pulled into a much larger conflict involving a secret cult called the Cult of Kosmos, who are secretly pulling the strings of the war from the shadows.

Your journey takes you across all of Ancient Greece, from the streets of Athens to the battlefields of Sparta, as you uncover the truth about your family and your destiny.
